Never Stop Challenging Yourself

Born and raised in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, I grew up in a midsized town close to some bigger ones - an hour driving to Philly, two to DC and three to NYC. We had four seasons there, and I thought snow was beautiful so I went to the University of Rochester in upstate New York. I took the true liberal arts degree route, spending time studying english, biology and biochemistry, working as a journalist, RNA researcher and EMT, before discovering and graduating in digital media studies. I enjoy thinking about how technology is impacting people in ways that we don't consider, and I try to spread awareness through digital art. Technology, particularly personal and mobile computing, has that unique quality of being an activity that's shared amongst billions of people in all parts of the world everyday. Such ubiquity has incredible potential to enact ideological changes across country lines. Together I believe we can develop a better future for everyone in the world.

I'm feeling... Galvanized!
